Privacy Policy for Smart Kharcha

Last Updated: February 2024

Thank you for using Smart Kharcha. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data Collection and Storage

Smart Kharcha does NOT collect any personal data. All your financial data including transactions, categories, and reports are stored locally on your device. We do not have servers, databases, or any external storage where your data is sent or stored.

2. Local Storage

The app uses local storage on your device (using Hive database) to store:

  • Your transaction records
  • Income and expense categories
  • App settings (language preference, theme)
  • Onboarding completion status
All this data remains on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

3. Permissions

Smart Kharcha may request the following permissions:

  • Storage Access: Only if you choose to export your data to PDF or Excel files. The app needs permission to create these files on your device's storage.
  • No Internet Permission: The app does not require internet access and does not have internet permission.

4. Data Export Feature

When you use the export feature to create PDF or Excel reports:

  • Files are created locally on your device
  • You have full control over these files
  • No data is uploaded to any server
  • You can share these files manually if you choose to

5. Third-Party Services

Smart Kharcha does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, advertising networks, or tracking software.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone, including children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
Email: support@smartkharcha.app
Note: As we don't collect any data, there's no data deletion process required. To delete all your data, simply uninstall the app or use the "Clear All Data" option in the app settings.

By using Smart Kharcha, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
