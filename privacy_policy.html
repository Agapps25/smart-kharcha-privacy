Privacy Policy for Smart Kharcha Last Updated: December 2025 Thank you for using Smart Kharcha. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information. 1. Data Collection and Storage Smart Kharcha does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All financial data including transactions, categories, and reports are stored locally on your device only. We do not operate any servers or external databases. 2. Local Storage The app uses local storage on your device (using Hive database) to store: • Transaction records • Income and expense categories • App settings (language preference, theme) • Onboarding completion status All data remains on your device and is never shared or transmitted. 3. Permissions Smart Kharcha may request the following permissions: • Storage Access: Required only when you choose to export data to PDF or Excel files. • Internet Permission: The app does NOT require internet access. 4. Data Export Feature When exporting reports: • Files are created locally on your device • No data is uploaded to any server • You may choose to share these files manually 5. Third-Party Services Smart Kharcha does not use third-party services, analytics tools, advertising networks, or tracking technologies. 6. Children’s Privacy Smart Kharcha is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. 8. Contact Us If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: Email: smartkharcha.app@gmail.com 9. Data Deletion As all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all data at any time by using the "Clear All Data" option within the app or by uninstalling the app. By using Smart Kharcha, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
